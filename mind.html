SPECULATIVE DESIGN

Mind washing

Team: Balella Ilenia, Baruzzi Pietro, Sabato Barbara & Zanibellato Angelica

Mind Washing is a speculative design project that proposes a future scenario in which we go about analyzing a world without communication between individuals.

Objective

In this scenario, we tried to understand what the new living condition of human beings might be, paying particular attention to the kind of intervention that design could make, exploiting the situation as an opportunity in creating new forms of communication.

PROCESSES

The environment where the story unfolds is a laundromat, the only place where interaction between people takes place. The senses of sight and hearing, in this context, awaken the emotionality of individuals and stimulate sharing.
